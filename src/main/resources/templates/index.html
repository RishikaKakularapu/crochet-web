About Me

Hi, I’m a developer by profession and a crocheter by passion.

I started crocheting as a way to step away from screens and explore a creative hobby beyond technology. Like many beginners, I learned through YouTube, one stitch at a time. In the beginning, it wasn’t easy — even crochet can be surprisingly tough on the eyes and requires patience.

But seeing each small piece come together into something beautiful kept me going. What started as a simple break from coding soon turned into a meaningful creative journey.

Today, every piece I create is made with care, creativity, and a love for handmade artistry.
